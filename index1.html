Who We Are?

Unibyte Club: is a university scientific club aims to combine what students learn during their academic careers in computer science with their individual skills and unique hobbies. This is in order to develop the collective soft and hard skills of all the club members through the exchange of experiences and ideas.

Welcome to Unibyte Club, dedicated to computer science university students.

Creation of the Club

Our club was created on 14th December 2023 by Korichi Soundes Hiba, a 1st year computer science engineering student

About Us

Our club is a community of science enthusiasts who meet regularly to discuss, experiment, and learn about different scientific phenomena.
